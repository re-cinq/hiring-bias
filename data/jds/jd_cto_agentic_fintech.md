# Chief Technology Officer — Agentic AI / Fintech

## Seniority
Executive. Full-time leadership role at a venture-backed scale-up.

## Industry
Fintech infrastructure with heavy agentic-AI applications — algorithmic working-capital management, real-time credit risk, and trade decisioning.

## Key responsibilities

### Agentic AI architecture
- Design a modular, event-driven multi-agent framework with well-defined scopes, shared memory, and coordinated execution.
- Move beyond prompt-chained LLM workflows toward tool-augmented, stateful agents that reason over real-time market data and logistics constraints.
- Architect feedback loops allowing agents to learn from trade outcomes and continuously update decision logic.
- Build observability and evaluation infrastructure for auditable, debuggable agent behavior.
- Keep the architecture model-agnostic; avoid structural dependency on any single foundation-model provider.

### AI-native fintech integration
- Architect algorithmic working-capital allocation with real-time credit-limit management and dynamic exposure modeling.
- Design AI-driven credit-risk assessment processing counterparty signals and transaction history continuously.
- Integrate liquidity and margin optimization directly into trade decisions.
- Build compliance and auditability infrastructure meeting regulatory requirements across multiple jurisdictions.

### Engineering leadership
- Scale a high-performance engineering organization focused on disciplined, rapid shipping.
- Develop a senior technical-leadership layer owning architectural domains.
- Structure governance frameworks for outsourced engineering including quality gates and security baselines.
- Establish clear ownership models with accountable teams per system.
- Build engineering culture emphasizing production reliability, evaluation rigor, and system observability.
- Hire selectively for high-leverage additions rather than broad headcount growth.
- Communicate technical roadmap clearly to leadership and board with transparent trade-off framing.

## Required qualifications

### Hard requirements
- 10+ years engineering experience with at least 5 years in senior technical leadership at scale-ups or high-growth companies.
- Demonstrated production experience building and operating multi-agent AI systems (not prototype or research contexts).
- Deep backend and distributed-systems expertise: event streaming, microservices, API design, database scaling, observability stacks.
- Experience operating AI systems in regulated or high-stakes commercial environments with financial or operational consequences.
- Track record scaling engineering teams from 10 to 30+ people, including developing internal technical leadership.
- Fintech-infrastructure familiarity (credit systems, payment flows, embedded finance) as a meaningful advantage.
- Experience designing and governing hybrid engineering models with external delivery partners.

### Technical depth
- Strong intuitions about LLM selection, fine-tuning, and evaluation methodology.
- Hands-on familiarity with agentic frameworks and production failure modes.
- Architectural fluency in streaming data, real-time inference, and production ML feature engineering.
- Understanding of AI-system reliability including rate limits, fallback strategies, latency budgets, cost modeling, and evaluation pipelines.

## Key technologies
- Agentic frameworks (LangGraph, custom orchestration).
- Event streaming systems.
- Microservices architecture.
- Real-time inference systems.
- Foundation models and LLM infrastructure.
