# Head of Development / Tech Lead

## Seniority
Senior / executive-level leadership role.

## Industry
IT consulting and software development — specialization in information management, open-source technologies, and digital sovereignty.

## Key responsibilities
- Provide operational leadership and oversight for all active and new client projects.
- Evaluate incoming client requests with rapid technical assessment.
- Develop initial architectural and conceptual designs.
- Lead and mentor a small interdisciplinary team of developers and consultants.
- Advise clients on architecture, technology, and security concerns for existing and new systems.

## Required qualifications
- Completed university-level degree.
- Extensive experience developing modern software systems.
- Strong background in IT architecture and technical project management.
- Strong understanding of and interest in open-source technologies, particularly in Linux/Unix environments.
- Proficiency in modeling and design, ideally UML expertise for data structures, application models, interfaces, and reference frameworks.
- Knowledge of requirements management and complex IT system design.
- Understanding of IT security, risk management, and compliance considerations.
- Strategic and analytical thinking with strong communication abilities.
- Willingness to quickly learn new technologies and domains.
- German language proficiency (minimum C1) and solid English skills.

## Desired technical skills
- Project management.
- Linux.
- Python.
- Architecture design.
- UML.

## Experience profile
Senior-level professional with a proven track record in software-development leadership, IT architecture, and team management.

## Employment details
- Full-time, permanent position.
- Remote-first working model with occasional domestic travel.
- Opportunity for annual performance/profit sharing.
- Long-term perspective with executive-level collaboration.
