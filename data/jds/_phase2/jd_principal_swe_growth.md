# Principal Software Engineer — Growth / Experimentation Systems

## Seniority
Principal / IC6 (Individual Contributor Level 6).

## Industry
Software development — cloud AI and developer tools, with focus on growth, experimentation, and measurement platforms.

## Overview
The role focuses on building AI-first growth and experimentation systems at enterprise scale. The position emphasizes designing foundational engineering systems for instrumentation, experimentation, rollout, and decision-making across products.

## Key responsibilities
- Own growth strategy through engineering excellence and systematic experimentation.
- Architect standardized experimentation frameworks including metrics, guardrails, analysis workflows, and rollout automation.
- Lead multi-workstream initiatives spanning teams and products (unified growth measurement, cross-surface funnels, experimentation quality).
- Build core capabilities: telemetry foundations, experiment assignment/targeting, feature flighting, and risk controls.
- Partner with Product, Data Science, Design, and Research teams to translate business goals into measurable systems.
- Remain deeply hands-on: write production code, review designs and pull requests, mentor engineers.

## Required qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or a related technical field.
- 8+ years of technical engineering experience with proficiency in languages such as C, C++, C#, Java, JavaScript, or Python.
- Proven ability to design and ship systemic solutions enabling multiple teams to work faster.
- Strong software-engineering fundamentals with production service or application experience at scale.
- Demonstrated technical leadership in architecture, service fundamentals, and quality aspects.
- Track record translating ambiguous problems into measurable engineering outcomes.
- High data aptitude with metrics, telemetry, and experimental analysis experience.

## Preferred qualifications
- 8+ years building high-availability features for multi-region, globally distributed systems.
- 4+ years of experimentation experience emphasizing quality, bias detection, and rollout-risk management.
- Experience building experimentation/measurement platforms used across multiple teams.
- Demonstrated use of AI tools to accelerate engineering velocity.
- Cross-team influence and stakeholder-alignment skills.

## Technologies and skills
- Programming languages: C, C++, C#, Java, JavaScript, Python.
- Experimentation platforms and feature-flagging systems.
- Telemetry and metrics infrastructure.
- Service reliability and distributed systems.
