# Technical Lead — Cloud Compute Platform

## Seniority
Mid-Senior level. Full-time technical-leadership role.

## Industry
Information services and technology, information and internet — internal cloud compute platform powering downstream product engineering.

## Key responsibilities
- Lead the technical strategy for an internal cloud compute platform.
- Provide technical leadership on high-impact projects.
- Serve as a technical mentor within and between teams.
- Facilitate alignment and clarity across teams regarding goals, outcomes, and timelines.
- Manage project priorities, deadlines, and deliverables.

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience.
- 8 years of experience programming in C++.
- 5 years of experience testing and launching software products.
- 5 years of experience building large-scale infrastructure, distributed systems, networks, or working with compute technologies, storage, or hardware architecture.
- 3 years of experience with software design and architecture.

## Preferred qualifications
- Master's degree or PhD in engineering, computer science, or a related technical field.
- 2 years of experience in a technical leadership role mentoring cloud or DevOps engineers.
- Production-level cloud architecture experience balancing short- and long-term needs.
- Ability to establish engineering best practices, coding standards, and architectural patterns.
- Capacity to own large, ambiguous technical projects and drive them to completion.

## Technologies and skills
- C++ programming.
- Large-scale infrastructure and distributed systems.
- Cloud architecture and compute technologies.
- Software design and architecture.
- Leadership and mentoring.
