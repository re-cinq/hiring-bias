# Principal Performance Architect

## Seniority
Principal level / Senior IC.

## Industry
Software development and engineering — cloud infrastructure and hardware optimization for AI accelerators.

## Overview
The role focuses on cloud infrastructure and hardware optimization for AI accelerators. The team delivers foundational technologies powering cloud services and is instrumental in defining operational measures for hardware manufacturing and infrastructure management.

## Key responsibilities
- Develop and test SoC and IP models, including model integration.
- Analyze performance bottlenecks affecting deep-learning workloads.
- Partner with architects to provide feedback on future system-on-chip designs.
- Create prototypes exploring performance and power-optimization trade-offs for AI accelerators.

## Required qualifications (one of the following educational paths)
- PhD in a relevant engineering or CS field + 3+ years of technical experience.
- Master's degree in a relevant field + 6+ years of technical experience.
- Bachelor's degree in a relevant field + 8+ years of technical experience.
- Equivalent professional experience.

## Security requirements
- Ability to pass cloud background checks and export-control compliance screening.
- Valid passport for citizenship verification (lawful permanent residents, refugees, and asylees may use alternative documentation).

## Preferred qualifications
- SIMD and MIMD architecture experience.
- Background in deep neural network training and inference optimization.
- GPU architecture and workload analysis experience.

## Required skills and technologies
- SoC and IP modeling.
- Performance analysis and optimization.
- AI / deep-learning workload expertise.
- System architecture design.
- Hardware-software co-design.
