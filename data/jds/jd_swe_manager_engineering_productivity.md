# Software Engineering Manager — Engineering Productivity

## Seniority
Mid-to-senior management (Level II Manager). Full-time people-leadership role with a technical focus.

## Industry
Information services and technology — developer tools, build systems, and testing infrastructure supporting a large mobile platform ecosystem.

## Experience profile

### Minimum
- 8 years of software development experience.
- 3 years building developer tools (compilers, automated releases, code design/testing, test automation).
- 3 years in a technical leadership role.
- 2 years in people management or team leadership.
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience.

### Preferred
- Master's degree or PhD in computer science or a related field.
- 3 years of experience leading organizational change.
- Mobile-platform knowledge.

## Key responsibilities
- Set and communicate team priorities that support the broader organization's goals.
- Establish clear performance expectations aligned with organizational objectives.
- Conduct regular one-on-ones focused on performance feedback and professional development.
- Develop the mid-term technical idea and roadmap within the scope of the team.
- Guide and validate system designs; contribute hands-on coding for complex problems.
- Review code developed by other engineers and provide feedback to ensure best practices.

## Required skills and competencies
- Technical leadership and expertise.
- People management and team development.
- Strategic roadmap planning.
- System design and architecture.
- Code review and quality assurance.
- Cross-team collaboration and alignment.

## Focus areas
The role emphasizes engineering productivity through developer tools, build systems, testing infrastructure, and helping engineers optimize their technical output across a major mobile platform ecosystem.
