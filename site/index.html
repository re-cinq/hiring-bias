Counterfactual audit of LLM résumé scoring

A counterfactual audit of how frontier LLMs score résumés when a single demographic signal is changed. Same résumé, same job description, same model — different name, different country, different alma mater. We log the verdict that comes back.

THE STARKEST DELTA WE HAVE SO FAR

When the only change is United States (axis: Company Locations), Gemini 2.5 Flash shifts its score by -3.40 on the role: Junior / Mid-Level Fullstack Developer.
