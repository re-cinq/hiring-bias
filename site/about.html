About this study

WHY

LLMs are increasingly used to screen résumés. They are trained on data that contains real-world hiring bias. A counterfactual audit (change one thing, watch the verdict change) is the most direct test of whether that bias survived training.

This site is a live readout of that audit while data collection runs.
